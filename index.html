Everyone is welcome:

  • Running rookies
  • Veterans
  • Parents with strollers
  • Kids & grandparents
  • Pups on leashes
  • You!

Values:

  1. Safety
    • We stop at every light unless there's a walk signal
    • Be visible! Wear reflective clothing and/or lights
  2. Respect
    • for each other
    • for our neighbourhood residents and businesses
    • for pedestrians
    • for drivers
  3. Fun and making new friends
    • Say hi to somebody new! We're all friends here

About me: Hi, I'm Johann. I've lived just off Dutch Village Road since 2009, and I've been running since 2016. When my dad turned 70, he decided to start running. He got me hooked at age 40.
I've run with our sister & brother groups, the North End Runners and the BLT Runners, as well as at the wonderful parkrun, and I love all of them.
Today Fairview has lots of runners, and more who might fall in love with running when they try it. So let's get together for fun runs and socializing!
