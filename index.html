Freeman's Fairview

Running rookies, veterans, strollers, pups on leashes, kids & grandparents, everyone is welcome.

Values:

  1. Safety
  2. Respect
  3. Fun and making new friends

About me: Hi, I'm Johann. I've lived just off Dutch Village Road for 15 years, and I've been running for half that time. When my dad turned 70, he decided to start running; he got me hooked at age 40. I've run with our sister & brother groups, the North End Runners and the BLT Runners, as well as parkrun, and I love all of them. Today Fairview has lots of runners, and more who might fall in love with running. So let's get together for fun runs and socializing!
